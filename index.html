Strategy Guide

Early Game Strategy (Levels 1-6)

  • Focus on farming jungle camps efficiently to reach level 4 quickly for your ultimate
  • Prioritize clearing small jungle monsters first, then move to larger camps
  • Look for ganking opportunities on overextended enemies in mid or side lanes
  • Avoid engaging tanks or fighters early - target squishy marksmen and mages
  • Use your first skill to poke and harass enemies from safe distance
  • Always keep an escape route planned before engaging any enemy

Late Game Strategy (Levels 13-15)

  • Play more cautiously as enemies will have defensive items and crowd control
  • Focus on picking off isolated enemies rather than engaging in full team fights
  • Use your mobility to split push and create map pressure
  • Wait for your team to initiate before diving into team fights
  • Target the enemy ADC or mage who can carry the late game
  • Consider building defensive items like Rose Gold Meteor if facing heavy magic damage

Advanced Pro Tips

  • Cancel your ultimate animation by immediately using basic attack after casting
  • Use S1 to check bushes safely before face-checking dangerous areas
  • Your S2 can be used defensively to escape over walls when in danger
  • Practice the "Sword Spike" combo in training mode for maximum burst damage
  • Always reset your S1 daggers before engaging to ensure maximum damage output
  • Use Retribution on jungle monsters to quickly clear camps and maintain farm advantage
  • Position yourself near walls during team fights for easy escape routes
  • Watch enemy cooldowns - engage when their crowd control abilities are down
